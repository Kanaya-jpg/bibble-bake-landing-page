About us: Bibble Bake "The Artisan Doughnut"

Bibble Bake is an artisan donut shop that originated in Bali in 2019 and has since expanded to Yogyakarta. Known for its beautifully crafted donuts, Bibble Bake Jogja offers a cozy and inviting atmosphere, making it a favorite spot for both locals and tourists. The café operates daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM and is located at Gang Kantil Pelem Kecut No.54, Karang Gayam, Caturtunggal, Depok, Sleman, Yogyakarta. Their menu features a variety of donut flavors, all made with high-quality ingredients to ensure a delightful experience for every customer.
